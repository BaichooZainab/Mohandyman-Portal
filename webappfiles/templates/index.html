Houseowner

A houseowner is a person who owns houses, apartments, all types of housing that they live in. But however, they might not have enough time to take much care for their housing because of family purpose or even for works. Thus, they seek for the helps of housekeepers to take care of their houses.There're different types of houseowners such as; Apartment, Single-Family, etc...

Housekeeper

Housekeepers are responsible for cleaning and reporting any safety hazards to the homeowner or manager in charge. They must complete tasks like vacuuming, sweeping, emptying trash cans, dusting shelves, cleaning windows, and mopping floors. Some Housekeepers change linens, wash dishes, and do light ironing and laundry.
